About Aqua Fern Boutique 🌿

Aqua Fern Boutique was created with a simple goal — to bring beautiful, home-ready plants directly to your doorstep. We believe plants transform spaces, improve well-being, and make every home feel alive.

Our mission is to make plant ownership easy, stylish, and accessible for everyone — whether you're a beginner or a plant enthusiast.
